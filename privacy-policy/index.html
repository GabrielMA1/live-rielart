Privacy Policy

The terms and privacy information that apply to this website and its inquiry tools.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Information we collect

RielArt collects information you voluntarily provide through the contact form, email, telephone, and scheduling tools. This may include your name, email address, company, project details, and meeting information.

How information is used

Information is used to respond to inquiries, evaluate project fit, prepare proposals, deliver agreed services, maintain records, and protect the website and business from misuse.

Service providers

The website may use Formspree to process contact-form submissions and Calendly to provide scheduling. Email, hosting, analytics, and other operational providers may process information on RielArt’s behalf under their own terms and privacy policies.

Cookies and local storage

The website stores your light or dark appearance preference in local storage. Calendly may set cookies or make third-party requests after you choose the booking tab. No advertising cookies are intentionally set by the static site itself.

Retention and security

Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the inquiry, project, legal obligations, and business records. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may ask about the personal information held in relation to your inquiry, request corrections, or ask for deletion where legal and contractual requirements allow.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to hello@rielart.com.
