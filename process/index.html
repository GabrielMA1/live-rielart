Process

A focused path from problem to handoff.

A predictable process makes it easier to give feedback, manage risk, and understand what is happening at every stage.

01
Discover & scope

Understand the business problem.

Review the audience, current experience or workflow, constraints, source material, tools, and the specific result the project should support.

  • Free email audit
  • Current-state review
  • Scope, assumptions, and dependencies
02
Strategy & brief

Choose the clearest route.

Confirm deliverables, platform or workflow choices, content responsibilities, milestones, and the review process before production starts.

  • Structured brief
  • Prioritized solution
  • Confirmed timeline and responsibilities
03
Build & test

Create the focused solution.

Design and implement the agreed system, test the important paths, and collect feedback through one organized review rather than scattered changes.

  • Focused production
  • Quality and usability checks
  • Structured revision round
04
Launch & handoff

Make the result usable after delivery.

Deploy the work, confirm ownership and access, document the essential steps, and identify the next improvement without forcing an ongoing retainer.

  • Launch or activation
  • Access and documentation
  • Walkthrough and next-step recommendations
Visible delivery

Follow the work through the RielArt Client Portal.

Once access is approved, clients can see project progress, tasks, target dates, client-visible updates, and support conversations without searching through scattered email threads.
